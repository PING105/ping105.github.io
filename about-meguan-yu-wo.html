从事影视广告行业六年，有幸与优秀的团队和工作伙伴合作，不断挑战自我，逐步从文案扩展至多项职能，如现场制片/副导演，成功参与并完成多个品牌项目，包括腾讯、招商银行、雷鸟 AR 眼镜、TT 游戏语音、深圳应急管理局、云南省科协、深圳市大鹏新区等。擅长影视项目全流程管控，包括时间线制定、预算管理、资源协调与风险应对，能够胜任快节奏、高标准的制作环境。

基于职业发展规划，我于 2022 年前往美国雪城大学（Syracuse University）攻读影视制作专业。在为期三年的学习过程中，深度参与二十余部短片项目，涵盖剧本开发、拍摄执行与后期制作等各个阶段，曾担任导演、副导演、摄影助理、现场收音、灯光师、剪辑师、调色师与声音设计等多个职位，积累了扎实的实战经验与全面的专业技能。

在校期间，担任了本专业摄影课程的助教（TA）以及学校影视器材库（The Cage）的管理员，系统掌握各类影视器材的使用与管理流程。熟练操作 DSLR 相机(Canon/Sony/Arri Mini)、灯光设备（LED-Apture/Arri Kit/HMI)与录音系统。在剪辑方面，熟练掌握多种后期制作工具，包括 Adobe Premiere Pro 剪辑、DaVinci Resolve 调色、Adobe Photoshop 与 Lightroom 调图，以及 Microsoft Office 办公软件。

可熟练使用 英文/粤语 作为工作语言。

With six years of experience in the film and advertising industry, I have had the privilege of working with exceptional teams and collaborators. I started as a copywriter and gradually expanded into multi-functional roles(producer & director assistant), successfully delivering campaigns for clients such as Tencent, China Merchants Bank, Rayneo AR Glasses, TT Voice, Shenzhen Emergency Office, Yunnan Association for Science and Technology, and the Dapeng New District in Shenzhen. I am well-versed in full-cycle project management — including timeline planning, budget control, resource coordination, and risk mitigation — and thrive in fast-paced, high-pressure production environments.

In pursuit of further professional development, I studied Film Program at Syracuse University (USA) from 2022 to 2025. Over the course of three years, I actively participated in more than 20 short film projects, contributing to script development, on-set production, and post-production. My roles have included director, assistant director, camera assistant, sound recordist, gaffer, editor, colorist, and sound designer, allowing me to build a well-rounded skill set across the filmmaking process.

In addition, I was selected as a teaching assistant (TA) for the VPA’s cinematography course and served as an equipment worker at the school’s production gear center, The Cage. Through these roles, I became proficient in the operation and maintenance of a wide range of filmmaking tools. My technical expertise includes DSLR camera operation, lighting setup, and sound recording, as well as post-production tools such as Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve for color grading, Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom for image editing, and Microsoft Office for project documentation and planning.
