作品集（1）

By Yuping Z

作为导演，我深知摄影技能的重要性，这不仅帮助我有效控制制作成本和时间，也提升了对影像质感的把控力。同时，我具备剪辑思维，能够精准调控画面节奏，增强叙事效果。各个课程项目中，我积极参与并承担后期制作工作，积累了丰富的实战经验。

熟练操作 DSLR 相机（Canon、Sony、Arri Mini）、灯光设备（LED Apture、Arri Kit、HMI）及录音系统。后期制作方面，精通 Adobe Premiere Pro 剪辑、DaVinci Resolve 调色、Adobe Photoshop 和 Lightroom 调图，以及 Microsoft Office 办公软件。

As a director, I understand the importance of cinematography skills, which enable me to better manage production costs and timelines while maintaining strong visual control.  also possess an editing mindset that helps me enhance the pacing and rhythm of storytelling. Throughout various course projects, I actively participated and took full responsibility for post-production, gaining extensive hands-on experience

Proficient in operating DSLR cameras (Canon, Sony, Arri Mini), lighting equipment (LED Apture, Arri Kit, HMI), and audio recording systems. Skilled in post-production tools including Adobe Premiere Pro for editing, DaVinci Resolve for color grading, Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom for image editing, as well as Microsoft Office.

片名：《苍耳
职务：编剧/导演
成果：入围 Lisbon Monthly Film Festival | Roma Prisma Film Awards | Nukhu (Archived) | Life-Off Filmmaker | Eurasia International Monthly Film Festival

出生在一个传统家庭的子苹，一直受到限制，难以追逐自己的摄影梦想。在坚持追梦的过程中，她意外发现了哥哥与那个她暗恋男孩之间的秘密

Born in a traditional family, Zi Ping was restricted to pursuing her photography dream. While continuing fulfilling her dream, she accidentally found the secret between her elder brother and the boy she adored.

 

片名：<Two Similar Strangers>
职务：编剧/导演/后期/调色
成果：参加2025 MFA Exhibitions & 2025 Brooklyn Waterfront Artists Coalition Exhibition，NYC

这部短片深受我信念的影响：生活中最微小的瞬间，往往承载着最深刻的情感。

This short movie is deeply influenced by my belief that the smallest moments in life can hold the most profound emotional weight. I aim to create films that resonate with audiences on an emotional and intellectual level.

 

片名：<A NIGHT, A LIFE>


职务：编剧/导演/后期/调色
成果：获得2025年雪城大学VPA学院学生基金
           电影节投递中

A NIGHT, A LIFE 探讨新世代下家庭中男性成员对女儿所施加的压力。

A Night, A Life, delves into the subtle yet pervasive patriarchal pressures exerted by male family members on the daughter of the household.
